# Superstore-data-analysis
Superstore Data Analysis and Visualization

This project analyzes Superstore sales data to identify key trends and business insights related to sales, profit, customer segments, regions, and shipping modes. 

Tools Used
1. Python
2. Pandas
3. Seaborn
4. Matplotlib
5. Google Collab

Key Findings
1. Technology category generated the highest sales.
2. The West region recorded the highest sales, while the South region had the lowest.
3. Standard Class was the most profitable shipping mode.
4. Consumer customers contributed the highest number of purchases.

Based on these findings, recommendations
1. Improve Marketing efforts or Customer services in lowest-selling region.
2. Ensure that high-demand products are consistently kept in stock to avoid missed sales opportunities.
3. Offer discounts strategically, as the analysis suggest it will not affect the profit margin of products.
4. Standard Class is both the most profitable and most frequently used shipping mode. The company can focus on improving and promoting this shipping option to enhance customer satisfaction and profitability.
5. As we seen in the analysis the lowest selling segment is Home Office, it may be due to limited product range, less target audience in this segment or less targeted marketing then other segments.
